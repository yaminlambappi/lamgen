Our Privacy Commitment

At LamGen, we believe privacy is a fundamental right. Our tools are designed with privacy as the core principle, not an afterthought.

Your files never leave your browser

How We Protect Your Privacy

Client-Side Processing

All our tools run entirely in your browser. Your files, data, and activities never touch our servers.

No Data Collection

We don't collect, store, or process any personal information. No accounts, no tracking, no profiling.

No Persistent Storage

We don't use cookies or local storage for tracking. Any temporary data is only for functionality.

No Analytics or Tracking

We don't use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, or any third-party surveillance.

What We Don't Do

Collect personal information
Sell or share your data
Use tracking cookies
Profile user behavior
Share data with third parties

Technical Implementation

Our privacy commitment is built into our technical architecture:

Browser-Based Processing

JavaScript-based tools that process everything client-side using modern Web APIs.

No Server Uploads

File processing happens in your browser. No uploads to our servers required.

Open Source Transparency

Our code is open source and auditable for privacy compliance.

HTTPS Encryption

All connections use HTTPS with modern encryption protocols.

Temporary Data

Some tools may temporarily store data in your browser for functionality:

  • Session Storage: For current tool session only
  • Local Storage: For user preferences and settings
  • Memory Cache: For temporary processing data

All temporary data is stored locally in your browser and can be cleared at any time.

Third-Party Services

We may use minimal third-party services for essential functionality:

CDN Services

For fast delivery of static assets (no user data involved)

Font Services

For delivering fonts (no tracking capabilities)

Your Rights

Transparency

Full visibility into how our tools work and process data

Control

Complete control over your data and how it's processed

Access

Free access to all tools without registration or tracking

Portability

Take your data and workflows with you when you leave

Children's Privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

International Users

LamGen is accessible globally. We comply with applicable privacy laws including GDPR for European users.

Since we don't collect personal data, most privacy regulations don't apply to our service.

Changes to This Policy

  • Maintain our privacy-first commitment
